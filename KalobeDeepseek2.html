About Us

Kalobe Microfinance Empowerment Platform Limited is a financial institution dedicated to providing accessible financial services to underserved communities, particularly targeting low-income individuals and aspiring entrepreneurs. Our mission is to empower these individuals through microloans, financial education, and business development support, enabling them to achieve economic independence and improve their quality of life.

Vision

To be a leading provider of microfinance solutions that foster sustainable economic growth and empowerment within marginalized communities in line with Sustainable Development Goals.

Mission

  • Providing affordable microloans tailored to the needs of small-scale entrepreneurs.
  • Offering comprehensive financial literacy programs that equip clients with essential money management skills.
  • Supporting business development initiatives that help clients launch and grow their enterprises.

Our Services

Microloans

Small loans with flexible repayment terms aimed at helping individuals start or expand their businesses.

Financial Education

Workshops and training sessions focused on budgeting, saving, investing, and responsible borrowing.

Business Development Support

Resources and mentorship programs that assist clients in developing viable business plans and strategies for success.
